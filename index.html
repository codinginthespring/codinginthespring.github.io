The Art of Color and Light: Stained Glass

For anyone who loves stained glass or is seeking appreciation for the art, this is the page for you. Stained glass has been a visual marvel for centuries. Many of humanity's most beautiful designs include stained glass, and yet it is underutilized in modern architecture.

  1. The primary material in the creation of glass is silica, or sand, but ground metal oxides can be added to provide various colors.
  2. The oldest existing window that incorperates stained glass is a part of the Augsburg Cathedral in Germany, from the 10th century.
  3. Contrary to popular belief, it is believed that the Egyptians developed the art of glass coloring around 2700 BC. It is believed that they used it for jewelry and currency; it was only later that the Roman Catholics popularized its use for cathedral windows, especially during the churches created in the Middle Ages. (Though it has been found in civilizations across the globe for many centuries.)
